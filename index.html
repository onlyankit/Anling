TENSES

What is Tense? How many types of tense?


A tense is a form of the verb that allows you to express.

There are three types of Tenses....


PAST TENSE


    1. Past Indefinite Tense
      • subject + first form of Verb + predicate
        Example.. I wrote a book.

    2. Past Continuous Tense
      • Subject + first form of verb + ing + was/were + predicate
        Example.. I was writing a book.

    3. Past Perfect Tense
      • Subject + third form of verb +had + predicate
        Example.. I had written a book.


PRESENT TENSE


  1. Present Indefinite Tense
    • subject + First form of verb + predicate
      Example.. I write a book.

  2. Present Continuous Tense
    • Subject + first form of verb +is /am/are +ing +predicate
      Example.. I am writing a book.

  3. Present Perfect Tense
    • Subject + has /have + third form of verb + predicate
      Example.. I have written a book.


FUTURE TENSE


  1. Future Indefinite Tense
    • Subject+ first form of verb + will /shall + predicate
      Example.. I will write a book.

  2. Future Continuous Tense
    • Subject + first for of verb + will /shall+ be +ing +predicate
      Example.. I will be writing a book.

  3. Future Perfect Tense
    • Subject + third for of verb +will /shall+have +predicate
      Example.. I will have written a book.
